Licensing over the data source

Once readed the license of the Madrid's City Council (Ayuntamiento de Madrid) which its responsible is the General Directorate of Sustainability and Environmental Control (a sub organization of the Madrid's City Council) making both organizations the rightsholder of the data, we can elaborate the license we are going to use. The data will have a CC BY 4.0 license and add some statements that must be followed in order to be compatible with the original license. These statements are:

  • The data can not be denaturalized
  • It may not be indicated, insinuated or suggested that the Community of Madrid and the Madrid City Council participate on the project where the data will be used
  • Last modification of the data must be included

That being the case we add some conditions to the CC BY 4.0 license in order to use it in harmony with the Madrid City CouncilÂ´s one.
